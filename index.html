About

I am the Founder & CEO of Maxora AI, where I build AI products and research-driven technology. I hold a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University.

My research applies novel deep-learning ideas — especially meta-learning and exploration — to natural language generation, bridging GANs, diffusion models, and meta reinforcement learning. A common thread in my work is a scheduler / explorer–exploiter framework inspired by Meta Exploration that learns how to sample or schedule noise rather than fixing it by hand.
